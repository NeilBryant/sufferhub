The SufferHub Project

We're here to encourage fitness by making your exercise more rewarding. We help you use your workout data to have fun, stay motivated, and gain new insights. We believe exercise should be about more than just suffering!

      

This is an example of a HTML caption with a link.

Suffer

Do your workout of choice (cycling, running, hiking, walking) and capture your data automatically using our iPhone app. Or, upload the .FIT files you're already capturing from your Garmin fitness device!

Share

Share your progress with your fitness-minded friends and socialize with fellow SufferHub members. We are a community of fitness minded individuals that want to help each other improve their athletic performance, be healthier, and have fun doing it!

Score!

Your workout data is worth more at SufferHub - earn points in online competitions that add a new level of fun to your regular workout routine. It's the game you play when the workout is over!
